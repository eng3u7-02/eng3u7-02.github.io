About Carlen

Hello! I am Carlen, the maker of this website. Here you will find
(hopefully interesting) links to other sites, pictures, videos and texts
all of which are dedicated to helping you understand this modernist poem!
I hope you enjoy your stay!

Carlen loves to learn, read, and make jokes about IB. When time
permits, Carlen reminisces about the past, where the fond
memories of previous school years come to mind. Outside of
school, Carlen can be found playing musical instruments,
attending archery club, and volunteering at a senior home.
